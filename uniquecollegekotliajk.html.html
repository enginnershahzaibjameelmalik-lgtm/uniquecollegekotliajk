About Us — Unique Institute of Modern Sciences About Us – Unique Institute of Modern Sciences About Us – Unique Institute of Modern Sciences

Unique Institute of Modern Sciences was founded in 2008 under the devoted guidance of Atif Hussain Subhani. Over time, valuable contributions by Raheem Subhani and Tahir Subhani helped the institution grow steadily and gain repute. Today, under the leadership of Madam Asia as Principal, the institute stands proudly at Exchange Gali, Kotli. For nearly two decades, we have remained committed to providing quality education. During this period, we have had the privilege of educating about two thousand students — many of whom today serve in diverse fields across Pakistan and abroad. Our mission has always extended beyond academic instruction: we strive to shape well‑rounded individuals with strong character, intellectual depth, and a spirit of service. By nurturing potential, instilling ethical values, and cultivating a passion for learning, we prepare our students not just for examinations, but for a successful and meaningful future.

UIMS Goals

Goals of Unique Institute of Modern Sciences (UIMS)

Provide high‑quality academic education

Offer strong foundational learning (Matric, F.A, F.Sc, B.A, B.Ed) to enable students to excel academically and get into good colleges/universities.

Foster holistic development

Cultivate not just academic skills but also character, ethics, discipline, social responsibility, and leadership qualities.

Nurture individual potential

Recognize each student’s unique talents and encourage them to grow intellectually, morally, and socially.

Prepare for modern challenges

Equip students with knowledge, critical thinking, and life skills that help them succeed in a dynamic world, nationally and globally.

Promote lifelong learning & moral values

Instill a love for learning, ethical values, integrity, respect, and a spirit of service — so they contribute positively to society.

Provide caring and supportive environment

Ensure a safe, disciplined, and motivating environment where students can learn, grow, and build confidence.

Support community & social contribution

Encourage students to be socially aware, responsible citizens and contribute positively to their community and country.

Facilitate access to quality education

Aim to make quality education available to as many students as possible regardless of their background, helping them build their future.

UIMS Facilities

Facilities at UIMS

  • Well‑equipped Classrooms (clean, spacious, ventilated, good desks/chairs and whiteboards)
  • Library & Reading Room — a library stocked with books, reference materials, periodicals for study and research.
  • Science Laboratories — separate labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology with lab apparatus for practical experiments.
  • Computer / IT Lab — computers and internet access for digital skills and assignments.
  • Sports & Playgrounds / Physical Activities Area — space for games, sports, physical training and recreation.
  • Canteen / Tuck‑shop — hygienic meals or snacks available during school hours.
  • Administrative & Staff Facilities — offices for administration and staff for smooth management.
  • Hygiene & Basic Amenities — clean washrooms/restrooms, safe drinking water, good lighting/ventilation.
  • Support Services — guidance/counseling room, first‑aid or medical support arrangements.
  • Flexible / Extra‑Curricular Facilities — for projects, group work, arts or other non‑academic activities.
UIMS – Future Aims

Future Aims of UIMS

  • Keep improving academic standards — continuously update curriculum, teaching methods and assessment standards so students stay competitive and ready for higher education or professional fields.
  • Incorporate modern learning methods & technology — adopt digital tools, computer/IT labs, smart classrooms, and e‑learning resources to prepare students for a modern world.
  • Focus on 21st‑century skills — beyond textbook knowledge: critical thinking, problem solving, creativity, communication, collaboration, digital literacy — to groom students for future challenges.
  • Promote holistic development & values‑based education — emphasize character building, discipline, social responsibility, integrity and cultural values alongside academics.
  • Provide inclusive & equitable education — make quality education accessible to students from varied backgrounds, ensuring fairness, opportunity and support for all.
  • Encourage extra‑curricular development & well‑rounded growth — through sports, arts, clubs, community service, and moral education — helping students grow personally, socially and physically.
  • Build a strong, professional faculty and staff team — recruit and retain qualified/trained teachers and staff, and invest in their development to ensure high quality teaching and institutional growth.
  • Establish modern infrastructure & facilities — expand and improve classrooms, labs (science, computer), library, activity spaces to support enhanced learning and extra‑curricular programs.
  • Prepare students for the global & modern world — with awareness of global changes, adaptability, digital skills, and moral grounding — to make them competitive nationally and internationally.
  • Cultivate lifelong learning and social responsibility — instill values of continuous learning, ethical citizenship, community service, and contribution to society.
